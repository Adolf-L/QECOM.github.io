Behind the Great Firewall, China has developed a unique walled garden of social networks, apps and media platforms.

how people use them.

Douyin

Douyin is China’s most popular short-form video app. Douyin reached 150 million daily active users in June 2018, and was the world's most downloaded app in the first quarter of 2018, with an estimated 45.8 million downloads.

Quickeast Network

No registration or activation processes required. Immediately start advertising to target users through email, text and video (Douyin).

WeChat

WeChat is China’s #1 most popular app and social network with over 1 billion MAUs where users spend ⅓ of their mobile time browsing.

Toutiao

Toutiao is China’s top daily news app integrated with a suite of fast-growing mobile apps owned by its parent company. As of August 2017 Toutiao had 600M users and 120M DAUs allowing advertisers to insert themselves into the daily reading habits of Chinese netizens utilizing interest-based categories.

Baidu

Baidu is one of the largest internet companies in the world, and the leading search engine in China, positioned and often referred to as the ‘Google of China’. It holds over 70% of the total Chinese search market share and has 660 million monthly users.

Weibo

Sina Weibo is one of the most popular social networks in China. As of March 2018, Weibo had 411M MAUs and 184M DAUs. Weibo functions as the primary one-to-many distribution platform for online content in China and is the platform where many KOLs grow their fanbase (even for other social networks). Weibo’s ad algorithm further amplifies organic content reach and engagement through the social graph of target users.

Tencent Network

Advertising through Tencent Ads Platform (WeChat's parent company) allows us to distribute ad posts across a long list of Tencent channels, including QQ News, Tencent Display Network (where we can specifically target inside education apps), QZone(632 million MAUs in 2017 Q1 ) - QQ messenger’s equivalent of a feed.

Youdao

Youdao ads platform is an ad platform under Netease, one of China’s top internet technology companies, it covers 1,000+ top apps, 1 billion users amount and 500 million DAUs (Daily active users) with over 10 billion daily exposures. Among these apps is Youdao Dictionary, the top mobile dictionary app in China, as well as other English study and education oriented apps.
